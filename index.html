Marketing Analytics with Google BigQuery

This project analyzes e-commerce event data (4.2M+ GA4 events) using Google BigQuery and visualizes insights with Google Sheets.

Insights

  • Traffic Sources: Organic & direct drive the most revenue.
  • Devices: Mobile has the highest conversion rate (~1.67%).
  • Products: Hoodies & sweatshirts dominate sales (Zip Hoodie: $13.8K revenue).
